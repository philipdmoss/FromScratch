In the pursuit of truth and justice...

Hi, my name is Philip and I am a rising senior at Duke University. Right now, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Sociology. After Duke, I hope to begin integrating my knowledge of technology with my passion for social work and economic justice. The topics that I am most interested in studying are data science, sociology, community organizing, and STEM education.

Currently, I am a fourth year scholar in the Robertson Scholars Leadership Program ready to embark on my Launch Summer as a Data Lake Engineer for GE Energy Connections.

On campus, I participate in the Digital Durham Interdisciplinary Team and serve as the chair of the Jazz@ Committee. In my spare time, I enjoy playing tennis, watching How to Get Away With Murder, talking with friends, and tweeting about anything that comes to mind.
